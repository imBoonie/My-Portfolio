Hi, my name is Dave Jones. I'm an aspiring dev from the UK.

 

 

 

 

WHO AM I?

I'm a mid 30's guy that decided to change my career. So I quit my job working in a freezer and decided that "I want to be a developer". Looking back I realise I had very little idea of what a developer does.

After bumbling my way through more than a few tutorials I was becoming frustrated with my progress, I felt like I was learning syntax but not problem solving. I read some advice that said words to the effect of "Build things and follow less tutorials". By now I had learned the basics of HTML and CSS and had been struggling with JavaScript for a while but thought I'd give it a go, so I built my house calculator. I knew it was basic but I was immensely proud of putting theory into practice.

WHAT I'M DOING

I'm building things! What things?, I'm not entirely sure yet but it's my intention to maintain this page with things I've built until I reach my goals.

WHERE I AIM TO BE

My goal right now is simple. To become employed as a developer. It is a large goal but one I will continue to pursue until I achieve it.
